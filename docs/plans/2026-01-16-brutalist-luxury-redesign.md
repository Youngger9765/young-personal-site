## Design System

### Color Palette (Rich Earth Tones)

**Primary Colors**:
- `deep-brown`: #2C1810 (Headings, dark backgrounds)
- `forest-green`: #1A3A2E (Secondary backgrounds, sections)
- `warm-cream`: #F5F1E8 (Main content background)

**Accent Colors**:
- `amber-gold`: #D4A574 (CTA buttons, highlights)
- `bronze`: #8B6F47 (Secondary accents, hover states)
- `warm-orange`: #E87B35 (Special highlights)

**Neutral Colors**:
- `charcoal`: #3A3A3A (Body text)
- `stone-gray`: #6B6B6B (Secondary text)
- `light-stone`: #D4CFCF (Borders, dividers)

### Typography (Mixed Style)

**Display Font** (Headlines):
- Family: Inter Black (900) / System-UI Bold
- Usage: H1-H3, strong visual impact
- Sizes: 120px (Hero), 72px (H2), 48px (H3)

**Body Font** (Content):
- Family: Merriweather / Georgia (serif)
- Usage: Paragraphs, descriptions
- Sizes: 18px (base), 20px (large)

**UI Font** (Elements):
- Family: Inter Regular/Medium
- Usage: Buttons, labels, navigation
- Sizes: 16px (base), 14px (small)

### Spacing System (8px Grid)

**Tight**: 8px, 16px, 24px
**Standard**: 32px, 48px, 64px
**Bold**: 96px, 128px, 192px

**Philosophy**: Large whitespace for visual impact (Brutalist aesthetic)

### Component Styles

**Buttons**:
- Border: 3-4px solid
- Border Radius: 12-16px (squared, not fully rounded)
- Shadow: Hard shadow (8px 8px 0px rgba)
- Size: Large padding (px-12 py-6 for primary CTA)

**Cards**:
- Background: Warm cream
- Border: 3px solid deep-brown
- Shadow: Hard shadow (offset, not blur)
- Texture: Subtle paper-like texture

**Dividers**:
- Thickness: 3-5px solid lines
- Color: Deep brown or bronze
- Layout: Asymmetric placement (Brutalist)

## Brutalist Elements

1. **Thick borders** (3-5px) everywhere
2. **Hard shadows** (offset, not blurred)
3. **Asymmetric layouts** (not perfectly aligned grids)
4. **Bold typography** (900 weight headlines)
5. **Geometric shapes** (sharp, not organic)
6. **Large whitespace** (96px+ spacing)
7. **Monolithic blocks** (solid color sections)

## Luxury Elements

1. **Earth tone palette** (warm, sophisticated)
2. **Serif body font** (elegant, classic)
3. **Quality photography** (professional, well-framed)
4. **Subtle textures** (paper, linen effects)
5. **Generous spacing** (breathing room)
6. **Premium copy** (confident, not salesy)
7. **Gold accents** (amber-gold for CTA)

---

## Anti-Patterns (Avoid)

❌ Purple gradients on white
❌ Rounded-full buttons
❌ Inter/Roboto everywhere
❌ Soft drop shadows
❌ Generic card layouts
❌ Perfect symmetry
❌ Pastel colors
